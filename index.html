COOPERATIVE IN STRUCTURE

mycoopshop.com Cooperative Limited is a Trading Coop registered under the Cooperatives Act 1997.
Our Coop can return or distribute profits (or extra share capital) to its members;
Our Coop can perform commercial functions that an individual could not;
Our Coop does not put an upper limit on membership numbers;
Our Coop has a ‘1 member, 1 vote’ system.

COOPERATIVE IN RESPONSIBILITY

The Cooperative structure also includes access to a Registered Charitable Foundation.
Our Coop enables member businesses to pursue any and all opportunities to meet community needs via the Foundation;
Our Coop works with funding institutions to provide the working capital to empower our communities.
